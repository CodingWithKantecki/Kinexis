Instructions

Step 1: Get Ready: Stand comfortably in front of your camera. Make sure you have enough space to move safely and that there are no tripping hazards nearby!

Step 2: Follow Me! I will guide you through each movement step by step. Move slowly and carefully, just like I show you. Take your time and have fun tracking your progress!

Step 3: All Done? When you have completed all the movements, end the session to generate your professional, anonymized PDF report. You can share it with your clinic or insurance if needed.

Stay Safe: Remember, this tool is meant to be used under the supervision of a medical professional. Stop immediately if you feel pain, dizziness, or discomfort. This is not a replacement for medical advice; it is just a way to measure and track your progress.
