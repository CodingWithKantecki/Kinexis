Our Goals

At Kinexis, our mission is to create privacy-first computer vision tools that respect individual identity while enabling meaningful motion analysis.

We aim to:

  • Protect personal identity in motion and video data.
  • Empower clinics and researchers to gain insights without compromising privacy.
  • Demonstrate how responsible AI can coexist with innovation.
