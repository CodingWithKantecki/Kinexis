How to Use Kinexis

Follow these simple steps to track your physical therapy progress with precision

1

Set Up Your Space

Find a well-lit area with 6-8 feet of clear space. Position your camera so your full body is visible from head to toe. Natural lighting works best for accurate tracking.

2

Start Your Session

Click "Start Session" and allow camera access when prompted. Our system will automatically calibrate to your position and begin pose detection within seconds.

3

Follow the Mascot

Watch our animated mascot demonstrate each exercise. Mirror the movements shown on screen. Real-time feedback will guide you to maintain proper form.

4

Track Your Reps

The system automatically counts your repetitions and measures your range of motion. Visual and audio cues help you maintain the correct pace and form.

5

Review Progress

See real-time metrics including rep count, range of motion, and form accuracy. Your progress is tracked throughout the session for comprehensive analysis.

6

Download Report

End your session to generate a professional PDF report. This includes exercise metrics, progress charts, and recommendations - perfect for sharing with your PT or insurance.

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • • Wear form-fitting clothes in colors that contrast with your background for optimal tracking accuracy
  • • Use a laptop or tablet for best experience - prop your device at chest height for ideal camera angle
  • • Start with slower movements to ensure the system accurately tracks your form before increasing speed
  • • Complete full sessions for most accurate progress tracking and comprehensive reports
