Our Mission

At Kinexis, our mission is to revolutionize physical therapy tracking by providing real-time measurements and professional progress reports in a fast, accurate, and accessible format. Patients can perform exercises at home while clinics monitor progress remotely, generating actionable insights instantly. Our automated demo and PDF reports make rehabilitation data clear, professional, and insurance-ready - without expensive or complex motion tracking systems.
